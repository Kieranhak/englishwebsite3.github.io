ipad rules in class:

  • The iPad should be kept flat on the desk at all times.
  • Students should close all apps at the end of each class
  • Only the apps specified by the classroom teacher are to be open during class periods.
  • As part of your homework, your iPad must be charged fully each evening, ready for class the following morning. You will not be allowed to charge your iPad in school.
  • The use of headphones or portable speakers with your iPad is strictly prohibited.
  • iPads should be switched to silent during class. You may only play audio sounds from your iPad when requested by the classroom teacher.
  • If the teacher is using the Apple Classroom app, Bluetooth and WiFi must be on.

    • What happens if the iPad rules are not followed?

  • 1) Student may not use an iPad. (Books)
  • 2) The iPad is kept in school during evenings.
  • 3) All apps other than e-books and a calculator are removed for an indefinite duration.
  • 4) iPad detention with restrictions for two weeks. (No apps can be added or deleted)
  • 5) iPad detention for a week (No Safari or Camera)

Ipad rules in class:

  • The iPad should be kept flat on the desk at all time's.
  • Student's should close all apps at the end of each class
  • Only the app's specified by the classroom teacher are to be open during class period's.
  • As part of your homework, your iPad must be charged fully each evening, ready for class the following morning. You will not be allowed to charge your iPad in school.
  • The use of headphone's or portable speaker's with your iPad is strictly prohibited.
  • IPads should be switched to silent during class. You may only play audio sound's from your iPad when requested by the classroom teacher.
  • If the teacher is using the Apple Classroom app, Bluetooth and WiFi must be on.

    • What happens if the iPad rule's are not followed?

  • 1) Student may not use an iPad. (Book's)
  • 2) The iPad is kept in school during evening's.
  • 3) All app's other than e-book's and a calculator are removed for an indefinite duration.
  • 4) IPad detention with restrictions for two week's. (No app's can be added or deleted)
  • 5) IPad detention for a week (No Safari or Camera)
